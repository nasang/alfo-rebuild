展望 2025

Pre-tax 401(k)

The 401(k) contribution limit for 2025 is $23,500 for employee salary deferrals, and $70,000 for the combined employee and employer contributions. – source

  • 公司的 match 部分应为 $23,500 * 0.5 = $11,750
  • 自投部分应为 $23,500
  • 2024年遗留可投资金额约为 $17,440.89

总计约 $52,690，按双周投资频率，每个周期约 $2,000，分配如下：

  • 70% VOO，– $1,400
  • 30% QQQM，– $600

HSA

The HSA contribution limits for 2025 are $4,300 for self-only coverage and $8,550 for family coverage. – source

  • 公司的 match 部分应为 $1,500
  • 自投部分为 $8,550 - $1,500 = $7,050
    • 若由公司从工资中代扣，每个 paycheck 为 $271.15
    • 若自己年初拉满，则损失约 $7,050 * 1.6% = $112.8 medicare tax。（详见美国 FICA 税详解）
